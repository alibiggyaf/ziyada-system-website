# Ziyada System Video Ad 01 (Arabic VO, No On-Screen Text, 16:9)

## Objective
Produce one brand-consistent video ad for Ziyada System:
- No on-screen text
- Arabic voiceover only
- 16:9 landscape format
- Saudi business context

## Format
- Duration: 30s
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Resolution target: 1920x1080
- Style: cinematic, modern, human-centric, business-realistic

## Brand Lock (Mandatory)
- Colors: #0f172a, #3b82f6, #8b5cf6, #ffffff, #e2e8f0, accents #06b6d4 and #ec4899
- Visual motif: geometric/3D network language consistent with Ziyada identity
- Tone: practical, empathetic, trustworthy, no hype
- Avoid western stock stereotypes and unrealistic growth visuals

## Single Idea
"من ضغط المتابعة إلى وضوح التشغيل"

## Scene Plan (No Text Graphics)
1. 0:00-0:06
- Saudi operations manager in a modern office handling fragmented channels (calls, chats, CRM).
- Mood: pressure, clutter, rapid pace.

2. 0:06-0:12
- Missed responses, delayed follow-up, team confusion.
- Camera: tight shots and quick cuts.

3. 0:12-0:18
- Geometric brand-style network visuals connect channels into one flow.
- Camera movement becomes smoother and more stable.

4. 0:18-0:25
- Team regains control: clear follow-up process, organized pipeline, calmer execution.
- Human-centric collaboration shots.

5. 0:25-0:30
- Subtle Ziyada logo reveal on deep-blue geometric background.
- No on-screen text; hold final shot for voice CTA.

## Arabic Voiceover Script (30s)
"كل يوم يبدأ برسائل كثيرة... ومتابعات ما تخلص.\n\n
تتأخر الردود، وتضيع فرص بين القنوات.\n\n
هنا يجي دور زيادة سيستم.\n\n
نوحد القنوات، ننظم المتابعة، ونخلي التشغيل أوضح لفريقك.\n\n
النتيجة: ضغط أقل، استجابة أسرع، وفرص أكثر تتحول لمبيعات.\n\n
إذا ودك شغل أذكى وأسهل، حنا جاهزين." 

## Performance Direction For VO
- Voice: Saudi-white Arabic, warm and respectful
- Problem section: slower with empathy
- Solution section: more confident and structured
- 0.5s pause between major ideas

"Create a 30-second cinematic business ad in 16:9 landscape (1920x1080). No on-screen text at all. Arabic voiceover will be added externally. Show a realistic Saudi office environment and business operators. Story arc: operational chaos to calm control. Scene 1: overloaded multi-channel communication and delayed follow-up. Scene 2: missed leads and team confusion, realistic stress. Scene 3: elegant geometric 3D network visuals in brand style unify channels into one process. Scene 4: organized pipeline, clear team collaboration, confident manager. Scene 5: subtle brand logo reveal on deep blue geometric background, no text overlays. Visual identity: modern, premium, human-centric, trustworthy, non-hype. Color direction: #0f172a, #3b82f6, #8b5cf6, #ffffff, #e2e8f0 with subtle accents #06b6d4 and #ec4899. Include culturally authentic Saudi wardrobe and workspace details. Avoid generic western stock style. Camera language transitions from tense handheld to smooth stabilized motion after transformation."

- Master: 1920x1080, 30fps
- Audio: Arabic VO front, ambient music low
