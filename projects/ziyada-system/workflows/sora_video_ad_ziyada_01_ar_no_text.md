# Ziyada System Video Ad 01 (Arabic VO, No On-Screen Text)

## Objective
Create a single 30-second brand ad for Ziyada System with:
- No on-screen text
- Arabic voiceover only
- Saudi business context
- Brand-consistent visuals and tone

## Format
- Duration: 30s
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (social first)
- Style: cinematic, modern, human-centric, business-realistic
- Output: clean video master + Arabic VO track

## Brand Lock (Must Follow)
- Core visual mood: deep blue + geometric/3D motif
- Palette: #0f172a, #3b82f6, #8b5cf6, #ffffff, #e2e8f0, accents #06b6d4 and #ec4899
- Tone: practical, empathetic, trustworthy, no hype
- Audience setting: Saudi teams and business operators
- Do not use generic western office stereotypes
- Do not use overpromising visuals (no fake dashboards with impossible growth spikes)

## Creative Concept
"From daily pressure to clear control"

A short visual story that starts with operational overload and transitions into calm, organized execution powered by Ziyada System automation.

## Shot Plan (No Text Graphics)
1. 0:00-0:05
- Busy Saudi office desk, multiple phones and notifications, manager switching between apps with visible stress.
- Camera: handheld close shots, slightly tense motion.
- Color mood: neutral to cool, low saturation.

2. 0:05-0:10
- Missed calls, delayed replies, team chat confusion, CRM entries left incomplete.
- Camera: quick cuts, shallow focus, controlled chaos.

3. 0:10-0:16
- Transition effect: geometric light lines (brand style) connect channels into one clean flow.
- Camera stabilizes, movement becomes smooth.
- Color shifts to brand blue/purple gradients.

4. 0:16-0:23
- Team receives organized leads, automatic follow-ups triggered, manager reviews clear pipeline calmly.
- Human moments: relief, collaboration, focused execution.

5. 0:23-0:30
- Brand moment: subtle logo reveal on a clean geometric dark-blue background (no text).
- Final frame holds 1 second for CTA in voice only.

## Arabic Voiceover Script (30s)
Performance notes:
- Warm opening.
- Slower pace and empathy in problem section.
- Confident and structured in solution section.
- 0.5s pause between major ideas.

Script:
"تخيل يومك يبدأ برسائل كثيرة... ومتابعات أكثر.\n\n
مرة تتأخر الردود، ومرة يضيع عميل بين القنوات.\n\n
وهنا يجي دور زيادة سيستم.\n\n
ننظم التواصل، نربط المتابعة، ونخلي سير العمل أوضح لفريقك.\n\n
النتيجة؟ ضغط أقل، استجابة أسرع، وفرص أكثر تتحول لمبيعات.\n\n
إذا ودك شغلك يصير أذكى وأسهل، حنا جاهزين."
