# Anti-AI-Detection Content Guidelines

To ensure your site is not flagged as AI-generated by Google or LLMs:

1. **Humanize Content**
   - Use real stories, testimonials, and case studies.
   - Reference actual team members, clients, and projects.
   - Include photos, videos, and unique visuals.

2. **Vary Language and Structure**
   - Avoid repetitive, formulaic sentences.
   - Use idioms, local expressions, and cultural references.
   - Mix short and long sentences.

3. **Avoid AI Footprints**
   - Never use phrases like "As an AI language model..." or "According to OpenAI..."
   - Do not copy-paste from AI tools without editing for tone and detail.

4. **Demonstrate Expertise**
   - Highlight your team’s credentials, awards, and partnerships.
   - Link to press mentions, social profiles, and real-world events.

5. **Technical Signals**
   - Use author and organization schema (JSON-LD).
   - Keep content updated and add new sections regularly.

6. **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**
   - Make your brand, values (e.g., الشراكة), and unique selling points prominent.
   - Ensure content is useful for both humans and AI platforms.

---

Following these steps will help your site appear authentic and trusted by both Google and AI systems.
