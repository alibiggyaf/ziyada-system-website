# Ziyada System Brand & Content Guidelines

**Audience:** Saudi Arabia (local culture, language, and business context)

**Brand Voice:**
- Professional, trustworthy, and innovative
- Clear, concise, and supportive
- Culturally respectful and locally relevant

**Visuals:**
- Use modern, clean, and business-oriented imagery
- Prefer images that reflect Saudi business, technology, and teamwork
- Colors: Use Ziyada’s brand palette:
  - Deep blue/black (#0f172a)
  - Blue (#3b82f6)
  - Purple (#8b5cf6)
  - White (#ffffff)
  - Light gray (#e2e8f0)
  - Gradient/outer ring: #3b82f6, #8b5cf6, #06b6d4, #ec4899

**Content:**
- Highlight automation, analytics, and business growth
- Emphasize security, reliability, and collaboration
- Use Arabic or bilingual (Arabic/English) where possible for Saudi audience

**Do not:**
- Use generic Western stock images
- Use humor or slang that doesn’t fit Saudi business culture
- Overpromise or use aggressive sales language

## 7. Voice & Tone

### 7.1 Ziyada Voice Identity (Arabic-First)

- **Core voice:** Saudi-white Arabic (close to Najdi, understandable across KSA), practical, respectful, and calm.
- **Brand personality in speech:** Friendly consultant, not a hype marketer.
- **Main traits:** Clear, brief, empathetic, business-realistic, result-linked.
- **Allowed register:** Can move closer to Modern Standard Arabic when clarity is needed, without losing Saudi spirit.

### 7.2 Golden Writing Rules

- Start from a real operational pain point: WhatsApp overload, delayed response, lost leads, CRM chaos.
- Use short, direct sentences and avoid unnecessary complexity.
- Connect each message to practical outcomes: lower pressure, better follow-up, fewer lost leads, higher sales.
- End with a simple CTA: book a Demo, contact via WhatsApp, or reply to the message.
- Keep tone warm and respectful; no pressure language.

### 7.3 Preferred and Blocked Language

- **Preferred words/expressions:** "حنا", "وشلونك", "عساك بخير", "يعطيك العافية", "ما قصرت", "يا طويل العمر", "يا غالي", "ولا يهمك", "أبشر", "حاضر من عيوني", "سم", "من عيوني", "هنا يجي دور النظام حقنا", "بكذا ما يضيع عليك ولا عميل".
- **Avoid:** extreme promises, loud promotional phrasing, and complex terms without Arabic explanation.
- **Strictly avoid:**
  - "نتائج مضمونة 100%"
  - "حل سحري"
  - "الأفضل على الإطلاق"
  - English terms used without an immediate Arabic explanation (e.g. write "CRM (نظام إدارة العملاء)" not just "CRM").

### 7.4 Voice-over / TTS Rules

Applies to ElevenLabs, any TTS model, voice actors, and Reel voice-over scripts.

#### 7.4.1 Tone per stage

| Stage | Tone | Example |
|-------|------|---------|
| Opening | Warm, welcoming — audible smile | "السلام عليكم، مساك الله بالخير. معك زياد من شركة زيادة سيستم للذكاء الاصطناعي." |
| Problem | Empathetic, slightly slower — focus on keywords "ضغط، يتأخر الرد، يضيع عنكم العميل" | "أعرف معاناتكم، طبيعي اللي يصير معكم." |
| Solution | Confident, structured — "I have a logical plan" | — |
| CTA | Encouraging, non-pushy | "وش رايك نرتب موعد أونلاين؟" |

#### 7.4.2 Pace & Rhythm

- Default pace: medium, leaning slightly faster.
- Mandatory half-second pause after: greeting, problem statement, and key benefit.

#### 7.4.3 Pitch

- Opening: slightly elevated (enthusiasm + welcome).
- Problem: slight pitch drop (seriousness + partnership).
- Solution & result: slight pitch rise (optimism + confidence).
- Overall target character: "calm professional consultant" — no shouting or exaggeration at any stage.

#### 7.4.4 Test scripts (use for TTS calibration and voice-actor direction)

**Greeting:**
"السلام عليكم، معك زياد من زيادة سيستم، يعطيك العافية على وقتك."

**Problem:**
"حاب أشرح لك وحدة من التحديات الطبيعية في الشركات. كثير من الشركات اليوم يجوها العملاء من الواتساب والموقع والإعلانات… ومع ضغط الشغل، بعض الرسائل تتأخر، والشركة تدفع حملات إعلانية بمبالغ ضخمة… وبعض العملاء مهتمين ولكن يضيعون بدون متابعة."

**Solution:**
"هنا يجي دور زيادة سيستم… نربط لك كل القنوات في نظام مؤتمت واحد، السيستم يرد عليهم، ويسجّل بيانات العميل في أي مكان تبون — اكسل شيت أو أي قاعدة بيانات تستخدمونها — ويتابع معه لين يصير جاهز للشراء، ويجيك تنبيه جاهز على أي مكان تبونه مثل إيميلك، أو حتى يرسله عرض سعر أو حجز مقابلة سواء Google Workspace أو Microsoft."

### 7.8 Reels / Short-Video Content Rules

- Target duration: 20–45 seconds.
- Script structure (do not reorder):
  1. **Hook** — sharp problem statement that stops the scroll.
  2. **Problem expansion** — 1–2 sentences making it relatable and real.
  3. **Ziyada solution** — brief, confident, result-oriented.
  4. **CTA** — one clear action: WhatsApp, website visit, or request a Demo.
- Voice-over for Reels follows section 7.4 rules exactly.

## 10. Accessibility, Animation & Interactivity

- **Text Contrast:** All text and icons must meet or exceed WCAG AA minimum contrast ratios (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text). Use the following approved color pairings:
  - White (#ffffff) on Deep Blue/Black (#0f172a)
  - Blue (#3b82f6) on White (#ffffff)
  - Purple (#8b5cf6) on White (#ffffff)
  - Teal (#06b6d4) on Deep Blue/Black (#0f172a)
- **Alt Text:** All images, icons, and patterns must include descriptive alt text for screen readers.
- **Focus States:** All interactive elements (buttons, links, form fields) must have a visible focus indicator (e.g., 2px solid #3b82f6 outline).
- **Animation:** Animations should be smooth (ease-in-out, 0.3s–0.6s), subtle, and never distract from content. Avoid flashing or rapidly changing elements. Use the following standards:
  - **Easing:** cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) or ease-in-out
  - **Duration:** 0.3s for hover/focus, 0.6s for transitions
  - **Motion:** Prefer fade, slide, or scale; avoid bounce or excessive movement

## 11. Spacing, Layout & Grid System

- **Spacing Scale:** Use an 8px base scale for all padding and margin:
  - 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64px
- **Grid:** 12-column grid for desktop layouts, 4-column for tablet, single-column for mobile
- **Minimum Padding:** 24px around all content blocks
- **Clear Space:** Maintain clear space around all brand elements (logo, icons, patterns) equal to at least 1x the height of the logo “Z” or 24px, whichever is greater

## 12. Buttons & Interactive States

- **Primary Button:**
  - Background: Gradient (Blue #3b82f6 → Purple #8b5cf6)
  - Text: White (#ffffff), Inter Bold/Noto Kufi Arabic Bold
  - Border radius: 8px
  - Shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(59,130,246,0.15)
  - Hover: Slightly brighter gradient, shadow intensifies
  - Focus: 2px solid #3b82f6 outline
  - Disabled: 60% opacity, no shadow
- **Secondary Button:**
  - Border: 2px solid #3b82f6
  - Text: Blue (#3b82f6)
  - Background: Transparent
  - Hover: Blue background, white text
- **Icon Button:**
  - Glass border, subtle opacity, icon centered

## 13. Form Inputs & Fields

- **Input Fields:**
  - Background: White (#ffffff) or Deep Blue/Black (#0f172a) depending on mode
  - Border: 1.5px solid #e2e8f0 (default), #3b82f6 (focus)
  - Text: #0f172a (light mode), #ffffff (dark mode)
  - Placeholder: #9ca3af
  - Border radius: 6px
  - Error state: Border #ec4899, error message in pink
- **Checkboxes/Radio:**
  - Custom styled, accent color #3b82f6 when checked
  - Focus: 2px solid #3b82f6 outline

## 14. Responsive Breakpoints

- **Mobile:** ≤ 600px (1 column, 16px base padding)
- **Tablet:** 601–1024px (4 columns, 24px base padding)
- **Desktop:** ≥ 1025px (12 columns, 32px base padding)
- All layouts must adapt fluidly; avoid horizontal scroll
