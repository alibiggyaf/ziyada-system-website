# ZIYADA SYSTEM — FINAL SERVICES STRUCTURE
Status: Final Draft
Ready for review, approval, and website implementation

---

## Final Recommended Public Service Names

1. Business Automation Systems
2. CRM & Sales Systems
3. Customer Acquisition Systems
4. Performance Marketing & Search
5. Smart Websites & Digital Platforms
6. Content & Social Media Systems

These names are stronger than broad terms like "growth" because they are clearer, more executive-friendly, and more aligned with a systems-led AI positioning.
