# ZIYADA — Real Worldwide Website References for Service Naming & Display

This file documents real websites whose service naming, structure, and presentation can inform Ziyada System's final website implementation.

## 1) Business Automation / AI Automation

### Automaly
URL: https://automaly.io/services/

What it shows:

- Uses compliance and trust markers high on the page.
- The presentation pattern leans on outcomes first, tools second.

- Lead with operational outcomes.
- Keep tool references secondary.
- Add trust signals near the top of the page.

### The AI Automation Agency
URL: https://theaiautomationagency.ai/

- Uses an advisory and roadmap framing.

What Ziyada can borrow:

- A phased roadmap section.
- Reassurance that automation improves team capability.

## Overall Strategic Conclusion

Across these real websites, the strongest common patterns are:
- Clear service titles
- Outcome-led hero statements
- Modular sub-services
- Proof elements near the top or mid-page
- FAQ or objection-handling section
- Strong CTA at the end of each service page
